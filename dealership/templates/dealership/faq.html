Frequently Asked Questions

How does SwiftDrive dealership work?

SwiftDrive Car Dealership offers a wide selection of new and used cars. You can browse our inventory online, schedule test drives, and even reserve a car. Our team is here to help you find the perfect vehicle to meet your needs.

How do I become a member?

Becoming a member is easy. Simply create an account on our website by providing some basic information. Once registered, you'll have access to exclusive member benefits, including special offers and early access to new arrivals.

How do you know what my car is worth?

We determine the value of your car using a combination of industry-standard appraisal tools and a thorough inspection by our experienced staff. Factors such as make, model, year, mileage, and condition all play a role in determining the value of your vehicle.

I have a problem logging in to My Pages. How do I fix this?

If you're having trouble logging in, first check to make sure you're using the correct email and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the 'Forgot Password' link to reset it. If you continue to experience issues, please contact our support team for assistance.

How can I cancel my membership?

To cancel your membership, please log in to your account and go to the 'Account Settings' section. From there, you can follow the instructions to cancel your membership. If you need further assistance, our support team is here to help.
